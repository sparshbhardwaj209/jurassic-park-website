Jurassic Park

Get ready for the ultimate Adventure

Relive the ultimate adventure! The Jurassic Park is the first ever theme park which gives you the experience to relive the original Jurassic Park and the blockbuster of 2015 , Jurassic World. It's a first ever theme park that provide you the experience of moving and roaring dinosaurs with more breathe-taking water and amusement rides.

Events

Museum

The museum is fun and informative. The museum has lovely visual displays that take you through the millennia of evolution, to extinction of the area and these magnificent fauna of that era. The audio visual presentations are very informative. There are number of major fossil in the museum. It will give realistic experience to the visitors. It will both knowledgeable and fun experience for school students as well as adults.

Rides

Amusement park in Jurassic Park has more then 20 rides and attractions to ensure that you can enjoy your fullest when visited with friends and family. The rides are divided into two segment kids and adults making the theme park safe and adventorous for school students. The rides include FRISBEE, ROLLER COASTER, GIANT WHEEL, OCTOPUS RIDE, SWING CHAIR, TORA-TORA, BUMP CAR and many more.

Water Park

We also have a well maintained water park with clean water having balanced pH and chlorine level. It include many thrilling and enjoyable water rides some of which are WAVE POOL, STORMY RIVER, SPACE BOWL, CRAZY CRUSIE, MAT RACER, AMAZON RIVER, DREAM SPALSH and many more. The water and designed in such a way that both kids and adults can enjoy them.
